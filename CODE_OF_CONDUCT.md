# Code of conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a welcoming, harassment-free community for everyone,
regardless of background, identity, experience, or level of technical
expertise.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Assume good intent while accepting responsibility for impact.
- Respect privacy and avoid publishing personal information.
- Help make the project accessible to new contributors.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized conduct, sustained disruption,
personal attacks, and deliberate exposure of private information are not
acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainer through the contact method
on their GitHub profile. Reports will be reviewed fairly and confidentially.
Maintainers may edit or remove content, issue warnings, temporarily restrict
participation, or permanently ban participants when necessary.

## Scope

This policy applies in project spaces and when someone publicly represents the
project.

Attribution: this policy is informed by the Contributor Covenant.
